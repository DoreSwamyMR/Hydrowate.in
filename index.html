About

The Vision Behind Hydrowate

We are reshaping the future of wastewater management through advanced engineering that prioritizes simplicity, long-term sustainability, and intelligence.

Dependable Sewage Treatment Plant(STP) That Safeguard Infrastructure and Build Trust


Hydrowate Enviro LLP is a Bengaluru-based company specializing in wastewater management solutions. We design, build, and manage systems that transform waste into reusable resources — reducing costs, ensuring compliance, and protecting the environment.


Our innovative approach eliminates conventional blowers and diffusers, delivering superior results with unmatched efficiency. We serve residential complexes, educational institutions, commercial spaces, and industrial facilities across India.

Our Technology

Deep dive into Bio-Jet SBR and Bio-Hybrid technologies — see how our systems outperform conventional treatment plants.

Bio-Hybrid — How It Works


A revolutionary hybrid treatment process combining anaerobic digestion, bio-tower aeration, and advanced filtration — delivering treated water with zero sludge and no minimum load requirement.


Key Advantages

  • ✓ Zero Sludge Generation
  • ✓ Easy Retrofit Potential
  • ✓ Biogas Recovery Option
  • ✓ No Blowers or Diffusers
  • ✓ Odor Control via Bio-Tower
  • ✓ No Moving Parts Inside Tanks
  • ✓ Very Low Power Consumption
  • ✓ Compact Footprint
  • ✓ No Minimum Load Requirement


Bio-Jet — How It Works


The Jet Aerator Treatment Process injects pressurized air and recirculated wastewater through a specially designed jet nozzle system, creating fine bubbles that ensure maximum oxygen transfer and efficient mixing.


Key Advantages

  • ✓ Compact & Modular Design
  • ✓ Low Cap-Ex and Op-Ex
  • ✓ Reduced Sludge Production
  • ✓ Less Space Required
  • ✓ No Moving Parts
  • ✓ Odor Control
  • ✓ No Maintenance Inside Tanks
  • ✓ No Noise or Vibrations
